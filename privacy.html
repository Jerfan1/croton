Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 29, 2026

Overview

Croton ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Key Point: Your workout data stays on your device. We do not collect, store, or have access to your personal workout information.

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Workout history and exercise data
  • Personal settings and preferences
  • Progress tracking information

Anonymous Analytics (Data Not Linked to You)

We collect anonymous, aggregated analytics to improve the app experience. This data:

  • Cannot be linked to your identity
  • Uses a random identifier (not your device ID or Apple ID)
  • Does not include any personal information

Examples of anonymous data collected:

  • App feature usage patterns
  • Workout completion rates (aggregate only)
  • App performance metrics

Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Your name, email, or contact information
  • Your Apple ID or device identifiers
  • Your location data
  • Your health or fitness data from Apple Health
  • Any data that could identify you personally

Third-Party Services

Apple App Store

If you purchase a subscription, the transaction is processed by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle payment data.

Analytics Provider

We use PostHog for anonymous analytics. Their privacy-focused configuration ensures no personal data is collected or stored.

Data Security

Your workout data is stored locally on your device using iOS's secure storage mechanisms. Since we don't collect personal data, there is no personal data at risk on our servers.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data: All your data is stored on your device and accessible within the app
  • Delete your data: Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data

Children's Privacy

Croton does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app does not require any personal information to function.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us
